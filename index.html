format_quote The Manifesto

Translating vision into wearable art.

I'm Fatoba Bamidele John — a fashion designer, creative director, and fashion stylist operating at the intersection of structure, style, and identity. I construct garments that don't just fit — they tell a story.

Based in Leicester City, UK, and working globally, I partner with clients, design teams, and production specialists to deliver projects from concept to completion. My approach fuses precise technical skill with creative expression — from custom bridal gowns to ready-to-wear collections.
